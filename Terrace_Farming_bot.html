Terrace Farming Bot

Inter IIT tech 2K19

Problem Statement

The teams are required to develop a lightweight robot that can do the work of plowing, seeding, watering, or harvesting considering the above-mentioned challenges for terrace farming. Robot should be able to perform at least one of the above-mentioned processes and able to climb up and down through the steps of terrace farming. Teams are required to produce a reliable navigation plan of the robot for autonomous navigation in the field.
