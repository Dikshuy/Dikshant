About me

Enthusiastic learner particularly interested in the field of robotics

I am Dikshant, pursuing my B. Tech, in Civil Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay. I am incredibly passionate about the field of robotics and control systems. I have worked on various projects related to it and still exploring this vast area to enhance my skills. Apart from it, I like sketching and making beats
