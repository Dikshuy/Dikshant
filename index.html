About me

Enthusiastic learner particularly intersted in the field of robotics

I am Dikshant pursuing my B.Tech,in Civil Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay. I am extremely passionate about the field of robotics and control system. I have worked on various projects related to it and still exploring this vast field to enhance my skills. I have represented IIT Bombay in inter IIT tech meet 2K19 for a high-prep task

I am presently working in the control subsystem of team Stride. I look after various controls of the quadruped, its optimal gait , inverse kinematics for proper stability and many more such tasks. I am also very active in technical activities and involved in Electronics and Robotics Club from my freshmen year. I fit well with this tech community
